Hello, I'm Harish P

Machine Learning Engineer | Poet

With a unique intersection of Machine Learning and Poetry, my journey unites technical prowess with a passion for creative expression. With experience in Machine Learning, I've worked extensively on projects that harness AI's power to create a change in the society. This fusion of technology and artistry allows me to push the boundaries of what AI can contribute to the arts, creating works that inspire and provoke thought about our evolving relationship with machines. My vision is to leverage AI to enhance poetic expression which empower writers and artists while preserving the human essence that makes poetry impactful. Ultimately, my work strives to reveal new possibilities for technology as a collaborator in the creative process. In the proccess, I also published my first book named "Epitome of Emotions" in the year 2023.
